Use a comma to delimit arguments in the command (Cmd) field for example [mpc,load,My Playlist]. GPIO pins are configured as Input/Pull-up and are 3V3. Pins GPIO2 and GPIO3 have fixed pull-up resistors. At power-on pins are set to input mode, GPIO0-GPIO8 are pull-ups to 3V3, GPIO9-GPIO27 are pull-downs to 0V. GPIO pinout diagram
